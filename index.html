About Ralco

Founded in 1994, Ralco Synergy Private Ltd. has grown from a single office in Bangalore to seven locations across India, backed by a team of 150+ professionals.

We are a trusted provider of IT hardware (rental & purchase), software license reselling, and electronic & electrical components–including desktops, laptops & servers, Cisco & networking devices, IoT & R&D components, and 50+ software licenses–backed by competitive pricing, a global supplier network, and timely deliveries across industries nationwide.

Our experienced team is committed to helping you find the right solutions, backed by quality products & exceptional service that keep your business running smoothly.
